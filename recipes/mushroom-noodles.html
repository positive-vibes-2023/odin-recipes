Holy Shiitake Mushrooms with Fresh Noodles!

Description

This simple, vegan-friendly stir-fry requires minimum effort, but delivers a truly flavoursome meal, with meaty mushrooms and mouthwatering soy and sesame sauce.

Ingredients

  • Vegetable oil - 1 tbsp
  • Garlic paste, or 2 garlic cloves, crushed and ﬁnely chopped - 1tbsp
  • carrot, peeled and sliced into 5cm (2in) batons- 1
  • red onion, thickly sliced- 1
  • fresh shiitake mushrooms, or 30g (1oz) dried, soaked according to packet instructions- 120g
  • light soy sauce- 1.2 tbsp
  • toasted sesame oil - 1 tbsp

Steps

  1. Heat the oil in a wok over a high heat.
  2. Add the garlic, onion, carrot, celery, mushrooms and mangetout (snow peas) or green beans and stir-fry for a couple of minutes, mixing well.
  3. Season with the salt and pepper, then add your noodles and the stir-fry sauce ingredients. Toss it all together over a high heat for 3 minutes so it’s nicely coated.
  4. Pile the noodles high on a large serving plate. Serve immediately for the mushroom lover(s) in your life.

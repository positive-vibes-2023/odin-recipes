Soya Rice

Description

The Soya sauce rice is an delicious dish which is not only quick and easy to prepare but it is also very nutritious.

Ingredients

  • Onion- 1
    • Mushrooms - 4
  • Green peas - 50 grm
  • Soya Sauce- 3 large spoons
  • Peanut butter= 2 large spoons
  • 1 cup rice

Steps

  1. Cut onion and mushroom in fine pieces
  2. Cook rice separately for 10 minutes
  3. Saute onion, green peas and mushroom
  4. Add 2 spoon peanut butter in the pan with onion and mushroom
  5. Add 3 spoon soya sauce
  6. Mixed cooked rice with sauted vegetables
